# Preface of the first translator, Bishop Juslenius.

For the benefit of my most beloved countrymen and the edification of their most precious faith and holy walk, there is published this small book, translated from the Swedish tongue, containing nevertheless all the parts needful unto salvation, with heartfelt prayer unto God, that He, for the sake of Jesus Christ our Lord, would grant it to be made unto the glory of His most high name, and unto the increase and strengthening of His poor congregation in grace! For although I dwell far off, set apart from the way, yet God is my witness that from the bottom of my heart I long after you all in Jesus Christ, and pray that your love may abound yet more and more in all knowledge and understanding; that ye may try what is best, and may be sincere, giving offence to none, until the day of Christ; being filled with the fruits of righteousness, which are wrought in you through Jesus Christ, unto the praise and glory of God. Phil. 1:8-11. Ps. 137:5,6.

Written at Skara, in Western Götaland, in the month of November, in the year 1745.

Daniel Juslenius

Bishop of the Diocese of Skara
